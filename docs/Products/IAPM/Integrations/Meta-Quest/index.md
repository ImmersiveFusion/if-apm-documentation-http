Connect your Meta Quest headset for a fully immersive APM experience.

## Supported Models

- Meta Quest 2
- Meta Quest 3
- Meta Quest Pro

## Requirements

- Meta Quest Link set up (wired or wireless)
- IAPM desktop client installed on your PC
- Quest controllers

## Setup

1. Set up [Meta Quest Link](https://www.meta.com/help/quest/articles/headsets-and-accessories/oculus-link/set-up-link/) following Meta's instructions
2. Launch IAPM on your PC
3. IAPM will automatically detect your Quest headset
4. Put on your headset and select IAPM from your Quest Link apps
