Connect your HTC Vive headset to experience IAPM in full VR.

## Supported Models

- HTC Vive
- HTC Vive Pro
- HTC Vive Pro 2

## Requirements

- SteamVR installed
- Vive headset and controllers set up
- IAPM desktop client

## Setup

1. Ensure SteamVR is installed and your Vive is configured
2. Launch IAPM on your PC
3. IAPM will automatically detect your Vive headset
4. Put on your headset to enter the immersive environment
