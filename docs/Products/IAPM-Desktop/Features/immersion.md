# Immersive Navigation

Enter the World of Your Application®. Immersive navigation allows you to step inside a live, spatial representation of your system rather than viewing static dashboards and fragmented data.

## Key Capabilities

### Visual System Exploration

Move through your application as if you were inside it. See services, flows, and dependencies react in real-time as you navigate the 3D space.

### Interactive Problem-Solving

Follow data paths, service interactions, and infrastructure impact with intuitive navigation. Anomalies appear as visual blockages - no need to decipher complex dashboards or query languages.

### Spatial Understanding

Leverage human spatial cognition to understand complex distributed systems. Relationships between services become physical distances you can traverse.

## How It Works

### Desktop Navigation

Use keyboard and mouse to fly through your application topology:

- **WASD** - Move forward, backward, left, right
- **Mouse** - Look around and aim
- **Scroll** - Zoom in/out
- **Click** - Select services for details

### VR Navigation

With a VR headset, experience true immersion:

- **Teleport** - Point and move to locations
- **Controllers** - Interact with services and data
- **Head tracking** - Natural look-around
- **Room scale** - Physical movement in your space

See [VR Headset Integration](../Integrations/index.md) for supported devices.

## Use Cases

### Troubleshooting Sessions

When an incident occurs, enter the immersive environment and navigate to the affected services. See error propagation visually and trace the path to root cause.

### Architecture Review

Walk through your system architecture with stakeholders. Show how services connect, where data flows, and identify potential bottlenecks.

### Onboarding

New team members can explore the production environment safely, building mental models of the system through direct experience rather than documentation.
