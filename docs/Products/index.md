# Products

Immersive APM is available in two editions to match your workflow.

## IAPM Desktop

The full immersive experience. A desktop application with 3D visualization and optional VR headset support.

[IAPM Desktop →](IAPM/index.md){:class="md-button md-button--primary"}

**Best for:**

- Deep-dive troubleshooting sessions
- Architecture reviews and planning
- Team presentations and war rooms
- VR enthusiasts

## IAPM Web

Browser-based access to your APM data. Manage your account, view dashboards, and configure settings.

[IAPM Web →](IAPM-Web/index.md){:class="md-button"}

**Best for:**

- Quick status checks
- Account and subscription management
- Team collaboration
- Mobile and tablet access
