## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

Upgrade at any time:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the change

Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing.

### Downgrading

Downgrade at the end of your billing cycle:

1. Go to **Billing** > **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the lower tier
4. Confirm the change

!!! warning "Downgrade Considerations"
    Before downgrading, verify:

    - Your node count meets the new plan's minimum
    - Data retention meets your requirements
    - Throughput limits accommodate your traffic
