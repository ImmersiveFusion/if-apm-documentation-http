# IAPM 1.15.0

Boop graduates from preview to a full in-world presence. Switch workspaces from voice or the console without leaving the world, navigate the new three-bucket radial menu (Help, Quick Read, Deep Analysis), and play with a proper screen-center cursor and right-stick orbiting on gamepad.

## What's New
- Voice and console workspace switching: say or type a workspace command to switch grids without opening the picker.
- Three-bucket Boop menu (Help, Quick Read, Deep Analysis) with workspace-aware Security and Research entries.
- Gamepad cursor with screen-center reticle for true in-world targeting, plus right-stick orbiting around the focal point.
- Boop idle behaviors: wandering, small skits, prop interactions, and chatter make long sessions feel less empty.
- GenAI Cost surfaced in trace context for at-a-glance spend visibility.
- Boop menu prefills the console for quick edits before send, and exposes an active-context diagnostic.

## Fixes
- Fixed Boop getting stuck in poses or tilted after rapid prop interactions.
- Fixed input-action bleed when the radial menu opened or closed mid-action.
