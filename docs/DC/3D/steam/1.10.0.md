# IAPM 1.10.0

This release introduces the live service graph, a real-time 3D visualization of service dependencies with animated traffic pulses. Also includes major performance improvements and a streamlined login experience.

## What's New
- Live Service Graph: See your services and their dependencies in real-time 3D with animated traffic flow and error highlighting.
- Demo Telemetry: Explore the product with built-in demo scenarios, no live data needed.
- Energy System UI: Energy bar showing your AI usage and tier capabilities.
- Streamlined login (no more separate browser window).
- Press Escape to interrupt Tessa without losing your prompt.

## Fixes
- Fixed rendering performance under high telemetry load.
- Fixed stability issues during long sessions.
- Fixed trace visualization scaling in the Diagnostics Room.
- Fixed lobby flickering during login.
- Fixed voice responses occasionally failing to stream.
