# Use Pro Tips

This guide shows how to turn the in-world Pro-Tips placards on or off and when to use them.

## Toggle Pro Tips

1. **Open the header.**
2. **Click the Pro Tips ON/OFF toggle.**
    - **ON** shows a placard next to every important object, a passive layer you read yourself.
    - **OFF** gives a clean expert view with no placards.

!!! info "What you see"
    With Pro Tips ON, a placard sits beside each important object, screen, and cube, explaining what it is and what to do with it. With Pro Tips OFF, those placards disappear and the space is uncluttered.

**Verification:** the placards appear or disappear across the whole grid as you toggle, not just on the object in front of you.

## Get help another way

If a placard is not enough, use one of the other two help paths.

1. **Click the smiley-face icon** to recall Mosey and ask him your question.
2. **Press `F12`** to open the Tessa console directly and ask your question.
