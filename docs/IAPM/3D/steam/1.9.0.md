# IAPM 1.9.0

Meet Tessa, your new AI Assistant. Ask questions about your telemetry data using natural language, via chat or voice, and get instant insights without writing a single query. This release also introduces the Diagnostics Room for immersive trace visualization, a redesigned grid picker, and new ways to explore the world.

## What's New
- AI Assistant (Tessa): Chat and voice-driven assistant for root cause analysis, performance exploration, and troubleshooting.
- Diagnostics Room: Explore traces and logs in an immersive 3D environment.
- Energy System: Track your AI assistant usage with a tier-aware energy bar.
- Grid Picker Redesign: Card-based grid selection with demo grid support.
- ProTips: Context-aware guidance that helps you discover features.
- WhereAmI Display: See your current room and location at a glance.
- Water Environment: Swim through the world for a new exploration experience.
- Teleportation overhaul with portal tunnel effects and warp animation.
- Overhead camera for top-down views.
- Initial macOS support (experimental).

## Fixes
- Fixed trace layout in the Diagnostics Room.
- Fixed avatar getting stuck after teleporting.
- Fixed portal teleportation issues.
- Fixed grid connection issues.
- Fixed room navigation context switching.
- Fixed voice interruption stalling prompts.
- Fixed crouching animation.
