# Benefactors

Benefactors are organizations and individuals who invest in our mission through financial contributions, technical expertise, or other resources. Your support accelerates our development and helps make spatial observability accessible to organizations of all sizes.

## Why Become a Benefactor?

Benefactor contributions directly impact our development:

| Contribution Type | Impact |
|-------------------|--------|
| **Financial** | Enables competitive pricing and free options for smaller organizations |
| **Technical** | Improves rendering, AI diagnostics, and platform capabilities |
| **Expertise** | Shapes industry-specific features and integrations |
| **Advocacy** | Builds credibility with skeptical industries |

## Benefits for Benefactors

### Recognition

Benefactors are recognized in the [Spatial Observatory](../Spatial-Observatory/index.md), the open space where the supporters who helped build this and the people who do the work are seen. Entry to the Observatory is open to everyone; recognition there is earned through the work, never bought through the size of a contribution.

### Strategic Advantages

| Advantage | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Market Differentiation** | Position your brand as an innovation leader |
| **Early Access** | Priority access to new features and roadmap influence |
| **Co-Innovation** | Collaborate on sector-specific solutions |
| **Networking** | Connect with other partners and early adopters |
