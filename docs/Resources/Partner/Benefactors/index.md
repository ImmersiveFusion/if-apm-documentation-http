# Benefactors

Benefactors are organizations and individuals who invest in IAPM's mission through financial contributions, technical expertise, or other resources. Your support accelerates our development and helps make 3D/VR APM accessible to organizations of all sizes.

## Why Become a Benefactor?

Benefactor contributions directly impact IAPM's development:

| Contribution Type | Impact |
|-------------------|--------|
| **Financial** | Enables competitive pricing and freemium options for smaller organizations |
| **Technical** | Improves VR rendering, AI diagnostics, and platform capabilities |
| **Expertise** | Shapes industry-specific features and integrations |
| **Advocacy** | Builds credibility with skeptical industries |
