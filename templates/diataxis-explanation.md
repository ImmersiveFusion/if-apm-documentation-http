Diataxis EXPLANATION template.

An explanation page helps the reader understand a concept. The reader is
not trying to complete a task or look something up. They want to know
how something fits together, why it was designed this way, or what the
alternatives are.

Write this template with:
- Discursive prose. Paragraphs, not bullets.
- Why over what. The reference page covers what; this covers why this
  design over the alternatives.
- Multiple perspectives allowed. "One way to think about this is X.
  Another is Y."
- Opinion is OK if labeled. "We chose X because..." is fine.
- No procedures. No "to do this, run..." If the reader needs to do
  something, link to a how-to.

An explanation page is the one place in the docs where narrative voice
and editorial perspective are welcome. Use that license.
